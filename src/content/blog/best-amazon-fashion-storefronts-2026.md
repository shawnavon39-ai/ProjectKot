## 4. What to Look For in a Fashion Storefront

Not all Amazon storefronts are created equal. The best ones have:

- **Organised categories** — you shouldn't have to scroll through 500 items with no structure
- **Regular updates** — fashion moves fast, and stale picks aren't useful
- **Honest descriptions** — creators who note sizing quirks or fabric quality save you returns
- **Mix of price points** — a good storefront has both splurge and budget options

## 5. How Amazon Affiliate Storefronts Work

When you shop through a creator's storefront, they earn a small commission from Amazon at no extra cost to you. It's how creators fund their content — and it means their recommendations tend to be things they've genuinely tested, since their reputation is on the line.
