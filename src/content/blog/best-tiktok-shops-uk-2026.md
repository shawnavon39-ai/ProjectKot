TikTok Shop has exploded in the UK. What started as a niche feature is now a full-blown shopping platform, with creators earning serious commissions from products they recommend in their videos.

Here are the best TikTok Shop creators worth following in 2026.

## Fashion

### StyleByMia
One of the UK's most popular fashion creators on TikTok Shop. Known for affordable high-street styling and seasonal capsule wardrobes. Her "outfit of the day" videos consistently go viral.

**Why follow:** Affordable fashion, great styling tips, consistent uploads.

### ThriftQueen
Sustainable fashion at its finest. ThriftQueen sources vintage and second-hand pieces, styling them into modern outfits. Her TikTok Shop features a rotating collection of pre-loved items.

**Why follow:** Sustainable fashion, unique finds, budget-friendly.

## Beauty

### GlowUpGuru
Skincare routines backed by actual ingredient knowledge. GlowUpGuru breaks down what's in each product and why it works, making her TikTok Shop recommendations genuinely trustworthy.

**Why follow:** Science-backed skincare, honest reviews, affordable picks.

### NailsbyLiv
The UK's go-to TikTok creator for nail art and nail care. Her shop features tools, polishes, and press-on nail sets at every price point.

**Why follow:** Nail art tutorials, affordable tools, creative inspiration.

## Tech & Gadgets

### TechFinds UK
Budget tech and gadgets that actually work. TechFinds UK tests every product before recommending it, and their TikTok Shop is curated rather than cluttered.

**Why follow:** Honest tech reviews, budget picks, no sponsored fluff.

## Home

### CozyHomeUK
Home organisation, cosy décor, and kitchen gadgets. CozyHomeUK's TikTok Shop is basically a wishlist for anyone renovating or redecorating.

**Why follow:** Home inspiration, practical gadgets, seasonal décor.
