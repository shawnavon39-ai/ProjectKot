## What Makes a Great Beauty Storefront

The best beauty storefronts share a few traits:

- **Tested products** — the creator has visibly used and reviewed the items on their social channels
- **Category organisation** — skincare, makeup, hair, and tools should be separated
- **Dupe callouts** — good creators flag when an affordable product performs like a luxury one
- **Ingredient awareness** — bonus points for creators who mention key ingredients and skin types

## How to Shop Beauty on Amazon Smartly

A few tips for getting the most out of Amazon beauty shopping:

1. **Check the seller** — make sure it's sold by the brand or Amazon, not a random third party
2. **Read the 1-star reviews** — they'll tell you about allergic reactions, sizing issues, or fakes
3. **Compare with Boots/Superdrug pricing** — Amazon isn't always cheapest for beauty
4. **Watch for expiry dates** — skincare products can sit in warehouses too long
