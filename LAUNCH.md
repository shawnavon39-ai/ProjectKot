# Pick Your Shop — V1 Launch Plan

## Launch Criteria (what "done" looks like)

- [ ] At least 5 real reviews on real shops
- [ ] 20+ products live on /deals with verified URLs
- [ ] Submit Shop flow tested end to end
- [ ] Submit Deal form live (done)
- [ ] Blog has 3+ SEO posts
- [ ] 404 page added
- [ ] Cloudflare Analytics or GA4 connected
- [ ] At least 3 creator shops with enriched pages (social links, long description)
- [ ] Email list has first welcome/deal roundup sent via Buttondown

## V1 Timeline (rough)

**Week 1 — Polish**
- Fix all deal URLs in admin
- Write 3 SEO blog posts (e.g. "Best TikTok shops UK 2026", "Top Amazon creator storefronts")
- Add 404 page
- Add analytics

**Week 2 — Content & Outreach**
- Enrich top 10 shop pages (long description, social links)
- Send 10 creator outreach DMs (see templates below → OUTREACH.md)
- Get 5 real reviews (ask friends/family to review shops they actually use)

**Week 3 — Launch**
- Announce on social (Twitter/X, TikTok, Instagram)
- Send first Buttondown email to subscribers (deal roundup)
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- Post in relevant subreddits (r/Frugal, r/TikTokShop, r/UKPersonalFinance)

## V2 Priorities (after launch)

1. **Price drop email alerts** — "notify me" on deal cards, Cloudflare cron job checks daily
2. **Creator claim flow** — verified badge unlocked when creator emails from their business address
3. **Weekly deal digest email** — automated Buttondown send every Monday
4. **Trending by velocity** — shops gaining clicks fastest surfaced on homepage
5. **TikTok video embed** on shop pages for TikTok creators
6. **"New this week" section** on homepage
