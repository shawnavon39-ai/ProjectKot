# Creator Outreach Templates

Target: micro-creators with 20k–500k followers who already have a shoppable presence.
Goal: get them to claim/verify their listing and ideally share it.

---

## Template 1 — DM (Instagram / TikTok)

> Hey [Name]! 👋 I'm the founder of Pick Your Shop (pickyour.shop) — a UK directory
> helping people discover creator shops all in one place.
>
> I've added your [platform] shop to the directory and would love for you to
> claim your listing and verify it so we can give you a verified badge.
>
> It's completely free — just takes 2 minutes. Happy to answer any questions!
> 👉 pickyour.shop/shops/[slug]

---

## Template 2 — Email (if business email is available)

**Subject:** Your shop is listed on Pick Your Shop 🛍️

> Hi [Name],
>
> I wanted to let you know I've added your [Platform] shop to Pick Your Shop
> (pickyour.shop) — a free UK directory where shoppers discover creator shops
> across TikTok, Amazon, Etsy, Instagram and more.
>
> Your listing is live at: pickyour.shop/shops/[slug]
>
> I'd love to give you a **verified badge** on your listing. If you're happy
> with it, just reply to this email and I'll get it sorted.
>
> If you'd rather not be listed, just say the word and I'll remove it immediately.
>
> Best,
> Shawn
> Pick Your Shop — pickyour.shop
